UNLOCK THE VALUE OF YOUR REAL ESTATE

Features

Immutable Ledger

Public blockchain based on Proof of Stake enables consensus based  immutable ledger which with progress of time can hold data that cannot be tampered with. Since it is distributed and decentralised there is no single entity that controls this. Hence you are more secure that a single authority be it government or corporate can tamper with the data.

Real Estate Auctions

We now enable partial ownership of prime real estate, so that it is easy for owners to release some of the value of their assets and use it for their personal purpose especially when they need it, without the necessity of selling off their entire asset.

Real Estate Deeds

We are creating a portal to create digital signature of documents signifying real estate deeds and issuing tokens on Cardano chain. These are the Non-fungible tokens for real estate, where a person can upload all his ownership documents and then use the tokens to monetize his hard asset when needed.

Roadmap

REIT is now going on a journey to unlock the value of real estate assets. Focus will be 

Conception and Formation
1

Cardano launches And we start creating a platform to issue NFT in real time

We want to democratise the world of finance using blockchain technology. All real world assets and exchange of value can happen via this platform, starting with tokenising of identity and assets. Now we do not need to rely on central authority to issue immutable assets. The trust is taken over by the chain and we now can find out innovative ways to interact with our real world assets. This NFT platform is the step in that direction with native assets as tokens being issued on Cardano.
2

Introduction of DID (Decentralized Identity) on chain 

Till now we have been relying on major social networks (like Google, Facebook, Twitter, Github, LinkedIn)and oAuth2 providers to provide us means to store our identity on the web and they have been using our information to track us everywhere. Now we will introduce a way to provide digital passport on Cardano via which the assets will be tied to the issuer. But now you will own. your identity and no one can use that identity for marketing and other purpose till you give them explicit consent.
3

Introduce integration with web based wallet such as Yoroi

We want to make it easier for corporates and individuals to protect themselves from fraud and for this easiest way is to store and interact directly with their wallets in an easy and hassle free way. It also removes the necessity of people to copy / paste their address on the site for getting their minted tokens, thus making the whole process fool proof and stress free.
4

PUBLIC ISSUANCE OF REIT TOKENS

Issue our own tokens and decentralise the entire application in order to enable the larger Cardano ecosystem participate in this revolution, especially the node operators who are helping on a daily basis to run the Proof of Stake blockchain ecosystem and enable them to generate more income in the process. It will also help the pool delegators to diversify into real world assets if they wish to participate in this ecosystem.
5

Smart Contract Introduction

Right now on chain Smart contract has not yet been introduced. We will use these contracts to enable the various marketplace activities such as auctions without the need of intermediary such as notary and other government entities.
6

Marketplace creation, Decentralisation & Further expansion

A creation of a marketplace where owners and investors can come together and exchange value in REIT tokens issued as part of NFT creation. A global decentralised ecosystem allows value exchange using real estate assets.
