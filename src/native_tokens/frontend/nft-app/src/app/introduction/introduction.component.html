Property owners

To the owners of property (real estate) who needs urgent liquidity  DoneReit provides a platform to easily leverage their real estate asset to interested and accredited investors without the need of intermediary like banks and do this via an easy to use self managed portal.

The key benefits of this system is.

No Banking intermediary

You do not have to go through banks for this purpose. We will be dealing with the KYC/AML requirements for the purpose and we will also have an insurance just like normal transactions in real estate have.

No risk of foreclosure or penalties.

If the house is owned by you, and you are auctioning a part of it for urgent financial requirements, your risk of totally losing your house is minimised and you will be able to regain control of the asset by repaying the capital with interest at later point of time.

Self managed portal with all the tools available.

We are continuously striving to make the customer be able to use the portal himself/herself, so that processes can run by itself with least about of external intervention. 

Investors

Diversify into Real estate

Till now investors could only invest into paper assets representing real estates. Now they can own a very specific real estate  that has been verified on this platform and provide an earning stream.

Marketplace for real estate

Leverage real time market data to place bids for desirable real estates. And since we can now own partial real estate and also can buy/sell the same in the marketplace it is a dynamic process that increases liquidity of the assets.

Global investment Opportunity

Investment now can be done without regards to border. Till now this was not possible, but because of the global cross border transfer of value enabled via this technology, we can now invest across in international markets in real time.

Roadmap

REIT is now going on a journey to unlock the value of real estate assets.

Conception and Formation
1

White paper & Development

We want to democratise the world of finance using blockchain technology. All real world assets and exchange of value can happen via this platform, starting with tokenising of identity and assets. Now we do not need to rely on central authority to issue immutable assets. The trust is taken over by the chain and we now can find out innovative ways to interact with our real world assets. This NFT platform is the step in that direction with native assets as tokens being issued on Cardano.
2

 Portal development

Business Portal development for onboarding customers for this platform will begin. Included in this work list will be many items ranging form introduction of DID (Decentralized identifier) as Atala Prism is released for developer use later this year and Integration of web wallet for storing tokens on Cardano ecosystem among other things.
3

Public Issuance of REIT tokens

Issue our own tokens and decentralise the entire application in order to enable the larger Cardano ecosystem participate in this revolution, especially the node operators who are helping on a daily basis to run the Proof of Stake blockchain ecosystem and enable them to generate more income in the process. It will also help the pool delegators to diversify into real world assets if they wish to participate in this ecosystem.
4

Smart Contract Introduction

Right now on chain Smart contract has not yet been introduced. We will use these contracts to enable the various marketplace activities such as auctions without the need of intermediary such as notary and other government entities.
5

Marketplace & Decentralisation

A creation of a marketplace where owners and investors can come together and exchange value in REIT tokens issued as part of NFT creation. A global decentralised ecosystem allows value exchange using real estate assets.


(All the above dates are estimates only and are liable to modifications based on market condition and status of the projects. As with any startups obstacles on the way can determine how we modify the roadmap)

Steps

1

Register Online

Person needs to register online and will undergo the required verification of his identity and KYC/AML checks as is legally required to buy/sell real estates. This holds for both buyers/sellers.
2

Register Real Estate

Register the real estate online including how much valuation you would like to have and percentage of the property you would like to sell. We will guide you through the registration process.
3

Auction Process 

Once the real estate is registered on the site other people interested can bid on the project partially. The owner can accept or reject the bid within a certain timeframe. Details of this process will be explained in FAQ
